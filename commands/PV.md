__Programmable VFO__

Set:

	PV p1,p2,p3

Get:

	PV p1

returns: p1,p2,p3

|p1|function|
|---|---|
|0|A band 118
|1|A band 144
|2|A band 220
|3|A band 300
|4|A band 430
|5|B band 144
|6|B band 220
|7|B band 300
|8|B band 430
|9|B band 1200

|p2|function|
|---|---|
|nnnn|Lower frequency in MHz. 4 digit

|p3|function|
|---|---|
|nnnn|Upper frequency in MHz. 4 digit
